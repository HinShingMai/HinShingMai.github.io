What To Do in the Experiment

In each session in the experiment:

1. You will use your cursor to control an arrow on the screen.

2. You must use the trackpad to control the arrow in this experiment. Do not control the arrow with your mouse.

3. Swipe left or right to swing the arrow's tail, which controls how fast the arrow turns.

4. Your goal is to follow the gray path as closely as possible.

5. Please do not click on or lift your finger off the trackpad unless you're finger is drifting off the edge.

6. There will be training and test sessions. During the latter, the top of the screen may be obstructed, limiting your view.

7. You will be presented with either a "blue" or a "red" arrow in each session.
